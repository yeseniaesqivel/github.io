Proyect description

Design and development of a gym management system as part of a university project. It is designed to manage all the processes of a gym, from user management to exercise equipment control. Key Features:

  • Authentication: Registration and authentication of gym users. It allows the creation of accounts for clients and staff so they can access their training information or their administrative profile, as applicable. Supports different user roles.
  • Appointment Management: Allows the registration of training and measurement appointments for clients, as well as rescheduling them if necessary.
  • Notification System: Sends reminders to clients about their upcoming appointments days before the scheduled date.
  • Exercise Equipment Registration and Management: Registers new exercise equipment and allows total administration of existing equipment in a flexible and intuitive way.
  • Exercise Routine Management: Registers personalized routines designed by instructors, also providing the ability to modify them easily and adapt them to the clients' needs.
  • Staff Management: Registers your staff efficiently and quickly.
  • Customized Visual Design: Use of colors and typography that reinforce the brand identity of the consultant, with subtle graphics and animations to capture the visitor's attention.

Proyect description

This project consists of the design and development of the frontend for an online fast food store. The page is designed to attract customers, effectively display the available menu, and provide a smooth and modern user experience that encourages online orders. Main Features:

  • Responsive Design: Optimization for mobile devices, tablets, and desktop computers, ensuring that users can browse and place orders comfortably from any device.
  • Interactive Menu Section: Visual presentation of the menu with high-quality images, detailed descriptions of dishes, and prices. Includes organized categories like burgers, pizzas, drinks, and desserts to facilitate navigation.
  • Strategically placed buttons like "Place Order", "Daily Offers", and "Contact Support", designed to maximize conversions.
  • Online Ordering Integration: Functional system to select products, add them to the cart, and proceed to payment, with customization options (such as choosing ingredients or sizes) and support for multiple payment methods.
  • Dedicated space to display special offers, popular combos, and discounts, with eye-catching design to grab the user's attention.
  • Basic SEO: Initial optimization for search engines, with the use of meta tags, semantic structure, and fast loading.
  • Section to display reviews and ratings from satisfied customers, reinforcing trust in the service.
  • Ability to offer the page in multiple languages, adapting to the local and international customer base.

This project consists of the design and development of a professional landing page for Dario Vargas, a photographer specialized in capturing unique moments and creating impactful visual experiences.   • Inspirational Blog: An optional section to share articles related to photography, stories behind the images, and tips for clients, helping to build an emotional connection with the audience.
  • Basic SEO: Initial optimization for search engines through the use of meta tags, semantic structure, and fast loading times to improve online visibility.
